About the app

My Journey is an app that can be used for business trips, holidays, backpackers and more any time you need to keep lots of important travel documentation in one place. In terms of timings, locations and any other information that you will need quick access to when your travelling. Register for a free account today.

How to use the app

In order to use the 'My Journey' application you will need to register an account. It's all free just type your details using the register form make sure you haven't created an account for our app before as you will be unable to register with email address which has already been used.
